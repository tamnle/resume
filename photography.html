What does hobby mean to me?

Work is important and essensial to every individual. However, work will not be enough to fulfill the entire life of a person. Through personal hobbies, we can easily understand quite a bit of that individual's personality.

To me, I believe that process is more important than result. Sometimes, the result may happen as unexpected. But, as long as we did everything under our best, we would never feel regret. So, I picked photography as my hobby because it can record the moments - which cannot be erased, like my efforts will never be forgotten.
